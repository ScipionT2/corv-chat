BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) lets you plug in your own API keys for providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. When you use your own keys, requests go directly to the provider — Nova just provides the interface. This means you're only charged at the provider's rate and can use any model they offer, independent of your Nova plan.
Yes. Your conversations are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to our servers. When using BYOK keys, requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider. We don't train on your data, we don't read your chats, and we don't sell your information. Privacy is a core design principle, not an afterthought.
When you cancel Pro, you keep access until the end of your current billing period. After that, you move back to the Free plan with access to 21 free models. All your chat history, settings, and custom themes are preserved — you just lose access to frontier models and priority routing until you re-subscribe.
