Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

01

Overview

Nova is privacy-first. Your data stays on your device. We built Nova because we believe AI should work for you, not surveil you.

02

Data We Collect

Minimal: basic analytics (page views only, no PII), optional crash reports. When using cloud models (Pro/Enterprise), queries are sent to providers per their privacy policies.

03

Data We Don't Collect

No personal information, no chat content on free tier, no tracking pixels, no cookies (except essential session), no advertising, no data selling.

04

API Keys

Stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Never transmitted to Nova servers. Sent directly to AI providers when you make a request.

05

Cloud Models

When using BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) models, your queries go directly to the provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.). Nova does not store, log, or process these queries.

06

Local Models

Free tier models run through Groq and SambaNova inference providers. These providers have their own privacy policies.

07

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq — inference provider
  • SambaNova — inference provider
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in
08

Data Deletion

All local data can be cleared through browser settings. Account deletion available via the Settings panel.

09

Contact

Questions about our privacy practices? Reach us at contact@nov-assistant.com.
